FAQ

Questions clients ask before we begin.

What kind of video work do you handle?

Brand films, product demos, explainers, motion graphics, short-form social content, GIFs, animation, and AI-powered production.

Do you handle the actual filming, or just editing?

Both. We direct and oversee shoots and handle the full post-production process in-house.

Why hire Zeluma Labs instead of a full-time editor?

You get production, editing, motion, sound, and AI workflows only when you need them, often for less than the cost of one salary across a full year.

What does AI-powered production mean?

For select formats, we use AI tools to generate or accelerate content without a traditional shoot, then apply creative direction and quality review before delivery.

How fast can you turn projects around?

Traditional shoots and edits are typically 2-4 weeks. AI-powered content can often be turned around in days.
